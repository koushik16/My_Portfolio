About Me

I'm Koushik Reddy Parukola, a PhD candidate with a passion for pushing the boundaries of Quantum AI and Bioinformatics. With a Master’s in Data Science from Indiana University, I am currently exploring advanced applications in Quantum Natural Language Processing and Cancer Research. My research interests lie in the intersection of quantum computing, natural language processing (NLP), and deep learning, with a focus on bioinformatics and healthcare applications.

Over the years, I’ve developed expertise in various machine learning techniques, from classical models like neural networks to quantum machine learning using Qiskit and PennyLane. My recent projects include developing quantum data encoding algorithms, error mitigation strategies, and cancer drug synergy prediction models. I am also deeply involved in analyzing the nuances of language using vector models and transformers.

Research

My research focuses on Quantum Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Bioinformatics. I explore innovative applications in Quantum Natural Language Processing and bioinformatics, aiming to advance fields such as healthcare and genomics.
